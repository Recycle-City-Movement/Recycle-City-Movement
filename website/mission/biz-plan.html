The Recycle City Movement Business Plan

Goal and Motivation

Create an non-profit organization of the highest moral integrity. Improve the lives of everyone through self-empowerment. Sponsor the initial creation of communities, which are themselves non-profit organizations, that follow the RCM community business charter and plan. Continue to sustain these communities through monthly and quarterly payouts.

Operation

Dealings are open and transparent. For example, meetings are recorded and shared with the public. Internal workings of the organization, such as external and internal communications, are visible from the website.

Labor

The organization is staffed entirely by volunteers. Responsible and productive volunteers get rewarded with greater responsibilities, such as job tasks and voting privileges.

Finance

Financial transactions are open and transparent. For example, the charity fund bank statement is posted on the RCM website on a monthly basis.

Reproducible Model

The business model is reproducible by others. The website reflects the openness of the business. Website source code is open-source. Business design and technology are documented with the website.

Assets

The business's only assets are the RCM website and its charity fund bank account.

Security and Privacy

The protection of the charity fund, member private information and credit card information is a fundamental responsibility of all staff members.

Technology

Promote the use and improvement of recycling technology by documenting and sharing the technology documentation used by communities and other potential entities.

Democracy

The organization control is done by elections and propositions. Members earn the right to vote by their commitment, that is to say by volunteering, and by their financial support of the RCM charity fund. How the business operates is in the hands of the volunteers and the fund supporters.
