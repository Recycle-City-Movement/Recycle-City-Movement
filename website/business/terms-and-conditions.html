The Active Status

In order to remain on the active status, a member must sign-in or be logged to the RCM website in the previous 30 days.

Classes

RCM classifies members by their level of responsibility. Responsibility is earned by taking an action that benefits RCM or a supported community. There are areas of responsibility that reflect the consequences of the action. These areas have impact on others and RCM. The measurement of this impact is how RCM ranks responsibility, and classifies, its members.

  • Fund Supporter

    Has made a provable donation to the RCM charity fund in the previous 30 days. Fund Supporters gain the right to vote on fund distribution proposals.

  • Tech Supporter

    Has contributed to the technology tree by submitting technology documentation that was reviewed and accepted by RCM admin or assistant staff.

  • Promoter

    Has publicized RCM in a positive light, and has shared the promotion on a RCM forum, and is recognized by a RCM forum moderator.
