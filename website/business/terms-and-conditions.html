The Active Status

In order to remain on the active status, a member must sign-in or be logged to the RCM website in the previous 30 days.

Classes

RCM classifies members by their level of responsibility. Responsibility is earned by taking an action that benefits RCM or a supported community. There are areas of responsibility that reflect the consequences of the action. These areas have impact on others and RCM. The measurement of this impact is how RCM ranks responsibility, and classifies, its members.

  1. Administrator (Admin)
    Was first nominated and then elected to fill one of the four adminstration departments. Each department has three Administrators. The total sum of Administrators is limited to twelve. The twelve Administrators are known as the Board of Directors.
  2. Community Founder
    Proposed and finalized an accepted RCM supported community.
  3. Assistant
    Was a Novice or Forum Moderator that was promoted by a RCM Admin. The number of Assistants is unlimited.
  4. Forum Moderator
    Is a Novice that has volunteered to work as a RCM Forum Moderator and was approved by a RCM Admin or Assistant. The number of Moderators is unlimited.
  5. Novice
    Has been approved as a RCM volunteer by RCM a Admin or Assistant. The number of Novices is unlimited.
  6. Community Member
    A member that belongs to a supported community with a visible profile on the community's website. Community Members and higher ranking members have the right to vote on fund distribution proposals.
  7. Fund Supporter
    Has made a provable donation to the RCM charity fund in the previous 30 days. Fund Supporters gain the right to vote on fund distribution proposals.
  8. Tech Supporter
    Has contributed to the technology tree by submitting technology documentation that was reviewed and accepted by RCM Admin or Assistant staff.
  9. Promoter
    Has publicized RCM in a positive light, and has shared the promotion on a RCM forum, and is recognized by a RCM Forum Moderator.
  10. Member
    Has completed the RCM sign-up process.
