Proposals

There are different kinds of proposals that members can create:

  • a new community
  • a charity fund donation
  • a change to a business document or process

Proposals go through phases:

  • During the first week a proposal is classified as a new proposal.
  • The proposal is then judged by the number of votes it has received.
  • If the proposal receives votes of at least 33% of its voter pool, it becomes active.
  • At this stage, if the proposal is not active, it is deleted from the ballot box.
  • The active proposal continues to remain in the ballot box for an additional week, receiving votes.
  • At the end of the two week period the fate of the proposal is determined.
  • If the proposal received more than 50% of the votes in its favor, then the proposal is adopted, and its implementation process begins.
